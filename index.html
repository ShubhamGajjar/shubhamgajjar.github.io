PUBG Mobile:

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG) is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean video game company Bluehole.

Information

Erangle Map

PUBG's Erangel is the game's first playable map, which is laid out on an 8km X 8km grid, making it four times larger than the recently released Sanhok map. With such a large playing area, one could strategize their drop to maximise their survival during the initial stages of the game.

Miramar Map

PUBG's Miramar is the second-playable map released by the company behind the franchise. Like Erangel, this map too is laid out on an 8km X 8km grid, giving the players enough freedom of choice to plan their approach to become the last man standing carefully.

Sanhok Map

Unlike the other PUBG maps, Sanhok is specifically designed so that the players on the map encounter a faster pace throughout the round. The map is 4km X 4km, which is four times smaller than the others offering an enhanced challenge.

Vikendi Map

The PUBG Vikendi map is a snow-covered region with plenty to offer players. It measures 6x6km, making it smaller than Erangel and Miramar (but larger than Sanhok), with multiple towns and urban environments spread between snowy plains and farmland.
